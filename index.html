Compact Conflict

The game lasts 12 turns. Once time runs out, the player controlling the most regions is declared the winner.

Temples

  • Each player starts with one temple under their control.
  • There are also neutral temples that you can conquer.
  • At the end of your turn, each of your temples produces a new soldier.
  • Temples can be imbued with elemental powers using upgrades.

Armies

  • You get 3 moves per turn. Each one allows to move one army to a neighbouring region.
  • An army that has conquered a region cannot move again this turn.
  • If you move into an occupied region, a battle ensues, with both sides dealing damage.
  • If you manage to kill all defenders, you take over the region.
  • If the defenders survive, your army is forced to retreat.

Income

  • Once you end your turn, new faith is generated: 1 for each region you control, and 1 for each soldier praying at one of your temples.
  • You also get 4 faith for each soldier killed in defense of a region.

Upgrades

  • Upgrades are purchased with faith. They grant special abilities.
  • There are 2 levels of each upgrade.
  • If a temple is lost, the upgrade is lost with it.
  • Instead of an upgrade, you can use faith to recruit soldiers. The more soldiers you recruit, the more expensive it gets.
